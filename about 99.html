Coach Quinonez-Robinson is a former D1 athlete who has competed in track and field for over 14 years. Her journey began with just one person believing in her — and now, she dedicates her life to being that person for others.

Track & Field is more than a sport; it’s a mirror of life. Through faith, discipline, and passion, Coach believes every athlete can rise — no matter where they start. With her unmatched patience and purpose-driven coaching style, she inspires others to not just run, but to fly.

“God is amazing. He’s the reason I can chase my dreams while guiding others to achieve theirs.”
