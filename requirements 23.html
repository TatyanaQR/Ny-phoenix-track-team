Team Requirements

🔥 Optional Trial Week: Experience what it’s like to be a part of the Phoenix family! You’ll have the opportunity to run or practice with the team for 2 to 3 days in a week to see if it’s the right fit for you.


🔥 Dedication & Determination: Being a part of the New York Phoenix Track Club is a commitment. This team is built on heart, hustle, and a deep belief in rising through adversity.


📝 Signed Contract: All athletes must review and sign the team contract before joining to ensure clear expectations.
