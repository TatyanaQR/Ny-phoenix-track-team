Who We Are

The New York Phoenix Track Club is more than just a team — we're a family. Founded on resilience, passion, and purpose, our club is a place where athletes rise through discipline, training, and mentorship. We build champions not just on the track, but in life.

Our Mission

To empower youth through elite track and field training, foster character, and ignite a spirit of perseverance that lasts a lifetime.

Our Legacy

Through hard work, dedication, and belief, our athletes have earned titles, broken records, and inspired communities. With every practice, we strive to uphold the Phoenix name — reborn, relentless, and rising again.
