About the Coach

"I didn’t get here by luck—I got here by faith, fight, and favor."

I wasn’t handed this journey. I had to earn it, pray through it, and rise through every setback. Track didn’t just shape my life—it revealed my purpose.

I started running because someone believed in me. That belief planted a seed—and that seed grew into a fire. A fire that carried me through every challenge, every doubt, and every finish line.

Track and field is more than competition—it’s a reflection of life. You fall. You struggle. But with enough heart, you rise.

There were times I questioned the path. But God always reminded me—“Be still. I am preparing you.”

He didn’t just call me to coach. He called me to lead, to mentor, to awaken something greater in others.

My mission is simple: to guide athletes toward purpose, strength, and the power of belief.

I’m a former Division I athlete who’s been running track since 2009. I graduated college in 2022, began coaching in 2023, and by 2025—God gave me the vision to build something of my own.

Hard work beats talent when talent refuses to work hard.
And I’ll believe in every athlete I coach… just like someone once believed in me.
