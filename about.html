About Us

New York Phoenix Track Club is more than a team — it’s a legacy in motion. Built on fire, faith, and discipline, we train champions on and off the track. Led by Coach Quinonez-Robinson, our athletes learn to rise from every setback and run with purpose.

Coach Quinonez-Robinson is a former Division I athlete with over 14 years of experience in the sport she loves. Her passion for track and field is matched only by her heart for people — especially those determined to grow, strive, and rise above. She believes in every athlete who shows up with hunger and heart, and her patience and diligence are what set her apart. She doesn’t just build runners — she builds resilient, motivated individuals ready to take on the world.

Whether you’re a beginner or a national competitor, there’s a place for you in the Phoenix family.
