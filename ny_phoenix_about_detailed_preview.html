About Coach Quinonez-Robinson

Coach Tatyana Quinonez-Robinson is a former Division I athlete with over 14 years of experience in track and field. She fell in love with the sport when one person believed in her — and that belief changed everything. It gave her the courage to chase her dreams, and today, she's devoted to doing the same for others.

For Coach Quinonez-Robinson, track and field is more than a sport — it’s a reflection of life. It takes time, discipline, and dedication to overcome trials and tribulations. But when you face those challenges with faith and resilience, you realize that there's always a rainbow after every storm.

New York Phoenix Track Club was built with that same belief — that everyone deserves a chance to rise. Under her guidance, athletes are not only trained to compete, but to grow into confident, focused, and resilient individuals.

Through every practice, every meet, and every moment in between, she leads with heart, patience, and purpose. And above all, she gives glory to God — because through Him, she’s able to chase her dreams and help others chase theirs.

"Built from the ashes. Running toward greatness."
