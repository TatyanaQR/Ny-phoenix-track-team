About Us

The New York Phoenix Track Club was born from grit, faith, and a passion for greatness. We rise from the ashes, stronger each time, ready to run toward victory.

Coach Quinonez-Robinson, a former Division I athlete with over 14 years of experience, brings unmatched heart and belief in her athletes. Her patience and diligence set her apart.

"Built from the ashes. Running toward greatness."

Team Requirements

All athletes must have a strong commitment to growth, discipline, and accountability.

Participation is open to youth athletes of all age groups. Athletes and guardians must sign the official team contract.

The contract includes meet fee policies, USATF membership, and team expectations.
