About the Coach

Coach Quinonez-Robinson is a former D1 athlete with over 14 years of experience in the sport of track and field. Her journey began with just one person who believed in her—and that belief became the fire that shaped her destiny. She pours that same energy into every athlete she trains. She loves working with those who are willing to work hard, dig deep, and most importantly—be better. For Coach Q, track is more than a sport—it’s a mirror of life. It takes time, faith, and patience to reach greatness. Setbacks happen. Doubt creeps in. But she teaches her athletes that through it all, God has a plan. “God told me to have faith and be patient. There is always a rainbow after every storm.” Her coaching is built on love, belief, and purpose—and that’s exactly what makes the New York Phoenix rise.
