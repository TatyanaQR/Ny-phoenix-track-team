Meet Coach Quinonez-Robinson

Coach Quinonez-Robinson

About the Coach

Coach Quinonez-Robinson is a former Division I athlete who has been running track for over 14 years. Her passion for the sport is rooted in her love for people and her unshakable faith.

She believes that track and field is a *mirror of life* — it takes time, discipline, and perseverance to overcome trials and tribulations. But once those storms pass, there is *always a rainbow on the other side*. Through coaching, she teaches her athletes that greatness isn’t given — *it’s earned through grit, grace, and God*.

Her journey began when just one person believed in her, and now she has made it her mission to be that person for others. With *patience, purpose, and power* in her words, Coach Quinonez-Robinson walks in faith and lights the way for the next generation.

“God is amazing. He’s the reason I can chase my dreams while guiding others to achieve theirs.”

Coaching Philosophy

“I coach with purpose, patience, and passion. To me, track and field is more than a sport — it’s a reflection of life itself. Every hurdle, every sprint, every finish line mirrors the struggles and triumphs we face in the real world. I believe in training the body, strengthening the mind, and feeding the soul.”

“My athletes don’t just run for times — they run with intention. I teach them that greatness is not handed out, it’s earned through discipline, faith, and unwavering belief in self. I will always be the coach who sees the potential others overlook, and I will push, protect, and pray for every young soul that steps onto my track. Because for me, coaching isn’t a job — it’s a calling.”
