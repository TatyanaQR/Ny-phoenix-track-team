Team Requirements 🏃‍♀️

  • ✅ You must be dedicated and willing to work hard through all training sessions, no matter your current fitness level or experience.
  • ✅ You must review and sign the contract within 7 days of receiving it in order to participate in practices and events.
  • ✅ You must participate in a trial week to see if the New York Phoenix Track Club is the right fit for you and your athletic goals.
  • ✅ You must have a renewed USATF membership with a verified DOB (Date of Birth) to ensure eligibility for all upcoming competitions.

🔥 For registration, please fill out the form on the contact page and you will be contacted immediately.
