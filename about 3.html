About Coach Quinonez-Robinson

God is amazing and has allowed me to chase my dreams and guide others to become successful.

I started running track because one person believed in me. That belief carried me through to success. Track and field is equivalent to life—it takes time and dedication to overcome multiple trials and tribulations. When we do, we understand that there’s a rainbow after every storm.

Coach Quinonez-Robinson is a former D1 athlete who has run track for 14 years. She loves the sport and is passionate about helping others succeed. She believes in everyone who has determination. Her patience and diligence set her apart from other coaches.
