Team Requirements

To be a part of the New York Phoenix Track Club, athletes and guardians must meet the following:

  • Maintain 85% attendance (practices and meets)
  • Have a current, valid USATF membership
  • Pay the $180 uniform fee via Zelle to (917)586-1769
  • Meet fees will be communicated before each event and must be paid by the listed deadline
  • Athletes are responsible for their own transportation to and from meets
  • Register through Athletic.net using the provided team code
