About Coach Quinonez-Robinson

Coach Quinonez-Robinson is a former Division I athlete who has been running track for over 14 years. Her journey began with one person believing in her — and that belief sparked a passion that carried her to success.

Now, she’s committed to being that light for others. With patience, discipline, and unwavering faith, she leads the New York Phoenix Track Club not just as a coach — but as a mentor, motivator, and guiding hand.

She believes track is more than just a sport — it’s a reflection of life. Through every trial and every triumph, athletes are taught how to rise, how to fight, and how to finish strong.

“It only takes one person to believe in you — and the rest, you’ll run with.”
Coach Quinonez-Robinson — Founder & Head Coach
