Team Requirements

Read Carefully Before Registering

  • Must be dedicated and self-motivated with a strong work ethic and a willingness to consistently improve.
  • Must review and sign the official team contract within 7 days of receiving it to confirm your commitment.
  • Must participate in a trial week to evaluate your fit, readiness, and enthusiasm for the training environment.
  • Must hold a renewed and valid USATF membership with a verified Date of Birth (DOB) to meet eligibility and insurance standards.

For registration, please fill out the form on the contact page and you will be contacted immediately.
