Team Requirements 💪🏽🔥

🌟 Optional Trial Week: Experience what it’s like to be a part of the Phoenix family! You’ll have the opportunity to run or practice with the team for 2 to 3 days in a week to see if it’s the right fit for you.

🔥 Dedication & Determination: Being a part of the New York Phoenix Track Club is a commitment. This team is built on heart, hustle, and a never-quit mindset. Only those ready to put in the work will rise with us. 💯

📝 Contract Requirements: All athletes must read, sign, and return the official team contract within 7 days of receiving it. Payment must also be submitted in full during this period in order to officially join the team.
